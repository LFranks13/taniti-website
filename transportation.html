Getting Around Taniti

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week. Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. The airport is being expanded so larger jets will be able to land in the next few years.

Taxi

Taxis are available in Taniti City for convenient island travel.

Bus

Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. Private buses cover the rest of the island.

Bike

Bikes and helmets are available to rent. Helmets are required by law and many streets are bike-friendly.

Walking

Taniti City is flat and walkable. Many tourists stay near Merriton Landing, easy to explore on foot.
