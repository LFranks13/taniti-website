Dining in Taniti

Taniti offers a flavorful dining scene that reflects its island roots and growing international influence. Visitors can enjoy everything from freshly caught local seafood to familiar American favorites and bold Pan-Asian cuisine.

Local Cuisine

Five restaurants specialize in traditional Tanitian meals, featuring fresh fish, rice, tropical fruits, and locally sourced ingredients.

American Favorites

Three restaurants serve American-style meals, including burgers, grilled dishes, and comfort foods familiar to many travelers.

Pan-Asian Cuisine

Two restaurants offer Pan-Asian cuisine, blending flavors from across Asia with fresh island ingredients.
