Discover the Island of Taniti

Small but full of surprises, Taniti is a tropical Pacific island spanning less than 500 square miles. Its landscapes are wonderfully diverse, from sandy and rocky beaches to a safe harbor, lush rainforests, and a mountainous interior featuring a small active volcano. Home to around 20,000 residents, the island once relied mainly on fishing and agriculture, though tourism has become an increasingly important part of the economy.
