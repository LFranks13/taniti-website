Stay on Taniti

Taniti offers a range of lodging options, from cozy cabins and boutique inns to luxury resorts. Each accommodation is carefully inspected and provides a safe, comfortable stay with island charm.

Hotel

Coral Bay Resort - Luxury rooms with ocean views, pools, spa, and fine dining for a relaxing island stay.

Family-Owned Hotels

Cozy rooms with friendly service, just steps from the beach and local attractions.

Bed and Breakfast

Charming, family-run guesthouses across the island, each offering cozy rooms and a freshly prepared breakfast.

Hostel

Budget-friendly lodging for adventurous travelers, with easy access to local beaches, hikes, and island experiences.
